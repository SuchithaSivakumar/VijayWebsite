DIVYA SASHA

Divya Sasha was born on 9 September 2005 to Vijay and Sangeetha. She is the youngest in the family and is studying in Chennai. Divya is very private, just like her mother, and rarely appears in public events. However, she got a lot of attention when she made a cameo appearance as Vijay’s daughter in the movie Theri (2016). Even though it was a very small role, fans were excited to see her on screen with her father. Divya is said to be good in academics and extracurricular activities, but unlike her brother Jason Sanjay, she has not shown any particular interest in cinema till now. Vijay and Sangeetha are very protective of her, and she is growing up away from media attention, enjoying a simple student life.

Childhood & Early Life:

Divya Sasha was born in 2005 as the second child of Vijay and Sangeetha. From her childhood, she has been very close to her father and mother. Unlike Vijay’s public life filled with fans and media, Divya’s childhood was kept very private. Vijay and Sangeetha always wanted her to grow up in a normal environment, away from the spotlight.

Appearance in Cinema:

Though Divya is not part of the film industry, she made a small but memorable appearance in Vijay’s movie Theri (2016), where she played the role of his daughter. That short cameo created a lot of buzz among fans, who were happy to see Thalapathy’s real daughter sharing screen space with him. After that, she has not acted again, as her parents want her to focus on her studies.
